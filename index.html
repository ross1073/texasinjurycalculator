How would you describe your injury?

This determines your pain & suffering multiplier — choose the description that best fits your situation.

Minor 1.5× multiplier

Soft tissue injuries, whiplash, bruising, or sprains. You have largely recovered or expect to fully recover within weeks. Minimal ongoing treatment needed.

Moderate 2.5× multiplier

Broken bones, herniated discs, concussion, or injuries requiring several months of physical therapy or ongoing care. Noticeable impact on daily life.

Serious 3.5× multiplier

Surgery was required or is recommended. Significant ongoing pain. Expected recovery is 6+ months. Some permanent effects possible.

Severe 4.5× multiplier

Permanent partial impairment. Major long-term disability. Disfigurement or scarring. Your ability to work or live normally is permanently affected.

Catastrophic 5× multiplier

Traumatic brain injury (TBI), spinal cord damage, paralysis, amputation, or other injury causing total permanent disability. Life has fundamentally changed.
