What Is Your Texas Injury Case Worth?

Fill in the details below and we'll estimate your settlement based on Texas personal injury law — including the 51% comparative fault rule.

The Texas Injury Settlement Calculator gives you an instant, free estimate of what your personal injury case may be worth — based on the same multiplier formula used by Texas attorneys and insurance companies. Enter your medical bills, lost wages, injury severity, and fault percentage and the calculator applies Texas's modified comparative negligence rule, including the 51% Bar Rule under Texas Civil Practice & Remedies Code §33.001, to estimate your potential recovery. Whether you were injured in a car accident, truck accident, motorcycle crash, slip and fall, or workplace injury, this tool walks you through each factor step by step — with guidance on what to enter if you don't have exact figures yet.
