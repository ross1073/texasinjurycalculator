Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit a contact form on Texas Injury Calculator, we collect: your name, phone number, email address, city, and any information you voluntarily provide in the message field. We also collect standard web analytics data (pages visited, time on site, browser type) through anonymized analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

Your contact information is used solely to connect you with licensed Texas personal injury attorneys for a free case review that you have requested. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails.

Who Receives Your Information

When you submit a contact form, your information is shared with one or more licensed Texas personal injury attorneys for the purpose of responding to your request for a free consultation. These attorneys operate independently.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months and then deleted. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@texasinjurycalculator.com.

Children

This site is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
